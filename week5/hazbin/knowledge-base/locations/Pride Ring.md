# Pride Ring

## Overview
Central layer of Hell where sinner demons reside; hosts Pentagram City and major Overlord domains.

## Governance
- Nominally under Morningstar authority (Lucifer and Lilith).
- De facto power shared among Overlords (e.g., Alastor, Vox, Velvet) and territorial gangs.

## Events
- Annual Extermination: Exorcists descend from Heaven to cull sinner population.
- Public broadcasts by Vox and other media Overlords shape public sentiment.

## Importance to the Hotel
- Location of the Hazbin Hotel and primary recruitment pool for redemption candidates.
- High population density offers diverse case studies for rehabilitation.
