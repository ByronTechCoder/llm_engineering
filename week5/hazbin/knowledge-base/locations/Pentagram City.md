# Pentagram City

## Overview
Primary urban center in the Pride Ring of Hell; hub for sinners, gangs, and Overlord territory.

## Features
- Neon commercial districts, black-market bazaars, nightclubs, and airship lanes.
- Turf controlled by Overlords and gangs; frequent skirmishes and bombings.
- Periodic Extermination raids from Exorcists descending from Heaven.

## Risks
- High collateral damage during turf wars (e.g., Cherri Bomb vs. Sir Pentious).
- Exploitation by Overlords, trafficking rings, and predatory contracts.
- Airborne hazards from Pentious airships and Vox tech.
