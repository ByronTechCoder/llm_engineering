# Hazbin Hotel

## Overview
Redemption-focused hotel founded by Charlie Morningstar to rehabilitate demons and reduce Extermination casualties.

## Facilities
- Lobby, bar, and front desk (run by Husk).
- Guest rooms for sinners seeking redemption.
- Kitchen and dining space, cleaned by Niffty.
- Broadcast-ready stage area for announcements and performances.

## Policies
- No exploitation or forced labor; participation is voluntary.
- Conflict de-escalation preferred over lethal force.
- Metrics tracked: sobriety streaks, violence reduction, cooperative tasks completed.
