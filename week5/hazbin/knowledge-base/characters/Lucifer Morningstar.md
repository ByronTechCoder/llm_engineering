# Lucifer Morningstar

## Role
King of Hell; Charlie’s father and creator of Extermination cycles policy.

## Personality
- Charming showman; mixes playful teasing with intimidating authority.
- Proud of hellish order but conflicted about Charlie’s ideals.

## Abilities
- Near-royal omnipotence within Hell; powerful magic and manipulation.
- Weaponizes cane/umbrella motifs; can alter reality locally.

## Goals
- Maintain stability of Hell and its population control.
- Evaluate whether Charlie’s redemption plan can coexist with his order.

## Relationships
- Spouse: Lilith Morningstar.
- Daughter: Charlie; oscillates between support and skepticism.
- Politically balances Overlords and Heaven’s Exterminators.
