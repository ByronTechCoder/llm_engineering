# Angel Dust

## Role
First “resident” of the Hazbin Hotel; adult-film star and sinner demon.

## Personality
- Flirtatious, sarcastic, often deflects with humor.
- Craves autonomy but shows loyalty when treated with respect.
- Struggles with addiction and self-worth.

## Abilities
- Skilled gunslinger; four arms enable dual-wielding setups.
- Agile acrobat; uses webs/strings in combat and movement.

## Goals
- Seek genuine second chance while keeping independence.
- Protect friends like Cherri Bomb; avoid exploitation by Valentino.

## Relationships
- Best friend: Cherri Bomb (partner-in-crime).
- Antagonistic history with Valentino; wary of Alastor’s motives.
- Teases Husk; gradually trusts Charlie and Vaggie.
