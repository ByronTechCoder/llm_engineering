# Alastor (The Radio Demon)

## Role
Overlord and radio-themed demon who “invests” in the Hazbin Hotel for amusement and influence.

## Personality
- Charismatic, theatrical, speaks with radio-era flair.
- Enjoys chaos but honors deals; playful yet threatening.
- Views redemption experiment as entertainment and leverage.

## Abilities
- Reality-bending via sound and broadcast motifs.
- Summons shadow creatures and uses vintage audio equipment as weapons.

## Goals
- Maintain amusement and influence over Hell’s power balance.
- Test Charlie’s idealism while ensuring his entertainment.

## Relationships
- Ally of convenience to Charlie; respects her optimism but treats it as a game.
- Rivalrous with other Overlords (e.g., Vox).
- Cordial but condescending toward staff like Husk and Niffty.
