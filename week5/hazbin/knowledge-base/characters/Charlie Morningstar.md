# Charlie Morningstar

## Role
Princess of Hell; founder of the Hazbin Hotel redemption project.

## Personality
- Idealistic, empathetic, relentlessly optimistic about rehabilitation.
- Conflict-averse but courageous when defending her vision.
- Values consent and autonomy; avoids coercive tactics.

## Abilities
- Royal authority in Hell; can command some demonic energy.
- Musical talent; uses song to persuade and inspire.

## Goals
- Prove that demons can redeem and reduce extermination cycles.
- Run a stable hotel that demonstrates measurable rehabilitation.

## Relationships
- Partner: Vaggie (manager and protector).
- Parents: Lucifer and Lilith Morningstar.
- Allies: Alastor (radio demon, investor), Husk, Niffty, Angel Dust.

## Risks
- Overreliance on external power (Alastor) for resources and protection.
- Faces skepticism from Overlords and the Exorcists.
