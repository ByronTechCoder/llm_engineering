# Niffty

## Role
Housekeeper at the Hazbin Hotel; hyperactive cleaning enthusiast.

## Personality
- Energetic, chipper, loves order and aesthetics.
- Curious and easily distracted by gossip or romance.

## Abilities
- Super speed and multi-tasking with multiple arms.
- Carries sharp cleaning tools; surprisingly capable in fights.

## Goals
- Keep the hotel spotless and welcoming.
- Find “nice people” and charming social circles in Hell.

## Relationships
- Recruited by Alastor; loyal to Charlie’s vision.
- Friendly with Husk and Angel Dust despite their vices.
