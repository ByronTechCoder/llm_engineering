# Husk

## Role
Gambler cat-demon running the Hazbin Hotel front desk and bar after Alastor’s bargain.

## Personality
- Cynical and gruff; hides reluctant compassion.
- Addicted to gambling and alcohol; dislikes being manipulated.

## Abilities
- Card-themed magic and limited flight via wings.
- Summons spectral cards/claws for offense and defense.

## Goals
- Pay off debts, avoid indentured service to Overlords.
- Keep hotel operations tolerable and guests in line.

## Relationships
- Bound to the hotel through Alastor; respects Charlie’s kindness.
- Banters with Angel Dust; tolerant of Niffty’s energy.
