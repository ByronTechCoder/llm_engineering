# Overlords of Pentagram City

## Overview
Powerful sinner demons who control major territory, media, or industry in the Pride Ring.

## Notable Overlords
- **Alastor (Radio Demon):** Reality-bending broadcast powers; currently backing the Hazbin Hotel for entertainment.
- **Vox:** Tech and media mogul; runs VoxTech networks and billboards; rivalry with Alastor.
- **Velvet:** Fashion and influencer-oriented Overlord; aligned with Vox.
- **Valentino:** Vice and adult entertainment Overlord; exploits performers like Angel Dust.
