# Exorcists

## Overview
Angelic enforcers who descend annually from Heaven to cull sinner demons during the Extermination.

## Capabilities
- Flight, blessed weaponry, and coordinated aerial assaults.
- Immunity to most demonic attacks; vulnerable to high-tier magic and Overlord-level force.

## Objectives
- Reduce sinner population in the Pride Ring.
- Maintain Heaven’s control narrative over Hell’s expansion.

## Relationship to the Hotel
- Hotel residents hope redemption can offer alternatives to extermination.
- Charlie seeks to prove rehabilitation outcomes that could influence future Extermination policies.
